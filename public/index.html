About Stand Up

Stand Up is a community-focused resource to help people recognize bullying, respond safely, and find support. We encourage respectful behavior and provide tools for survivors, parents, educators, and bystanders.

  • Recognize signs of bullying—verbal, physical, social, or online.
  • Respond safely as a target, witness, or supporter.
  • Report incidents to trusted adults or appropriate authorities.
  • Recover with resources for emotional support.

Quick Safety Tips

  1. Save evidence — screenshots, messages, names, dates.
  2. Block and mute the harasser on platforms where possible.
  3. Tell a trusted adult, teacher, or counselor.
  4. Report to the site/app using their safety/report tools.
  5. Seek emotional support — you do not have to face this alone.
